Dr. Parikh has also presented oral and poster presentations at various national and international meetings such as the American Academy of Allergy, Asthma and Immunology, American College of Allergy, Asthma, and Immunology, American Thoracic Society, Federation of Clinical Immunology Societies and World Association of Sarcoidosis and other Granulomatous diseases.
